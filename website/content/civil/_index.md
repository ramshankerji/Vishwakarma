All about Civil Engineering.

We intend to develop the following minimum feature set in our application for Civil engineering applications.

### Structural Elements
- **Line Elements**: Beams, Columns, and Walls (Polygonal, Ring, Arc)
- **Concrete Slabs**: Polygonal and Rectangular
- **Concrete Domes**: Ellipsoidal domes for specialized structures (e.g., nuclear)
- **Foundations**: Piles
- **Plates & Gratings**: Rectangular and Polygonal plates, Polygonal gratings
- **Connections**: Bolt Groups (Rectangular and Circular)

### Reinforcement
- Single Rebars and 3D Rebar Sets for complex geometry
- Couplers and Laps
- Surface Links (Link and Unlink)

### Code Checking
- **Indian Standards**: IS 456 (LSD & WSD), IS 13920, IS 3370, IS 800 (LSD & WSD)
- **International Codes**: ACI 318, Eurocode 2 & 3, GB 50010 & GB 50017, AISC 360

### Analysis & Simulation
- Load Cases, Load Combinations, and Self Weight
- Various Load Types: Point, Uniform, Trapezoidal, Pressure, Temperature, Time Series
- **Load Generators**: Wind (IS 875), Seismic (IS 1893), Snow (IS 875)
- **Supports**: Fixed, Pinned, Roller, Partial, Functional
- **Analysis Types**: 3D Frame, Static, Spectrum, Time History, Thermal Analysis
