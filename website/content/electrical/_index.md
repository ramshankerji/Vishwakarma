All about Electrical Engineering.

We intend to develop specific features for Electrical engineering applications in the future. Currently, our planned features focus on cross-disciplinary integration. 

### Planned Features
- **General 2D & 3D Drafting**: Using standard shapes, lines, text, and blocks to represent electrical layouts and single-line diagrams (SLDs).
- **Intelligent Parameterization**: Future integration of electrical equipment with embedded parameters (similar to mechanical pumps/compressors).
- **Interdisciplinary Coordination**: Ability to model electrical trays and conduits alongside piping and structural elements to perform clash detection and comprehensive 3D modeling.
- **Reporting & BOM**: Bill of materials extraction for electrical components (cables, trays, fixtures).
